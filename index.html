Biography

Ruiyang Ren is an M.S. graduate of Big Data Science and Engineering at Renmin University of China (RUC). His supervisor is Prof. Ji-Rong Wen and Wayne Xin Zhao. Prior to RUC, he completed his B.S. of Computer Science and Technology at Taishan College, Shandong University (2019). His current research interests include natural language processing and information retrieval.
