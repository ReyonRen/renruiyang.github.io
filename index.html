Biography

Ruiyang Ren is a 3rd-year Ph.D. student of Artificial Intellegence at Gaoling School of Artificial Intellegence, Renmin University of China (RUC). His supervisor is Prof. Wayne Xin Zhao. He completed his M.S. at School of Information, RUC (2022) supervised by Prof. Ji-Rong Wen and Wayne Xin Zhao, and B.S. of Computer Science and Technology at Taishan College, Shandong University (2019) supervised by Prof. Xuemeng Song and Liqiang Nie. His current research interests include natural language processing and information retrieval.
